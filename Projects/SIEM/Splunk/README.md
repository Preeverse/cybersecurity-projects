#  Splunk Cybersecurity Lab

A hands-on, compact Splunk project designed to showcase **log analysis**, **threat hunting**, and **SIEM skills** for your cybersecurity portfolio.

---

## Splunk
Splunk Enterprise is the data collection, indexing, and visualization engine for operational intelligence.

##  Splunk Projects
This lab demonstrates:

- [Installing & configuring Splunk Enterprise](/Projects/SIEM/Splunk/Splunk_Installation)
- Ingesting Windows, Linux, Sysmon, and Firewall logs
- Performing detection & threat-hunting analysis
- Creating dashboards, alerts, and correlation searches
